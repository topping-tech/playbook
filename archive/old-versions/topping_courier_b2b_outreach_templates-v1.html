Topping Courier - B2B Outreach Templates

Professional first-contact email and LinkedIn message templates for reaching B2B businesses and requesting a short in-person meeting.

Instructions for the Outreach Person

Before sending, personalize the placeholders carefully. Do not send the template without replacing the business-specific fields.

  • Replace [First Name] with the contact person's first name.
  • Replace [Business Name] if used in the subject or message.
  • Replace [industry/business category] with the correct category, such as food distribution, printing, apparel, electronics parts, or local product distribution.
  • Replace the sender details at the end with the correct name, position, phone number, email, and website.

Email Template

Subject:
Reliable Local Delivery Support for [Business Name]
Email Body:
Hi [First Name], I hope you are doing well. My name is [Your Name], and I am reaching out on behalf of Topping Courier, a GTA-based courier company supporting local businesses with same-day, next-day, scheduled, and recurring delivery services. Given your work in [industry/business category], we wanted to connect and see whether Topping Courier could support your pickup and delivery operations, especially if your business handles recurring orders, batch deliveries, customer shipments, or business-to-business deliveries. Our goal is simple: help businesses reduce delivery coordination, improve reliability, and make local logistics easier to manage as order volume grows. We would appreciate the opportunity to schedule a brief 15-minute meeting with you. One of our representatives can visit your location, learn more about your current delivery process, and explain how our service may fit your business needs. Would you be available sometime next week for a short meeting? Please let us know what day and time works best, and we would be happy to arrange it. Best regards, [Your Full Name] [Your Position] Topping Courier Inc. [Phone Number] [Email Address] [Website]

Recommended Personalization Examples

  • Food distribution: Given your work in food distribution, we understand how important reliable scheduled deliveries can be for customers and business partners.
  • Print shops: Given your work in printing and marketing materials, we understand that timely local delivery can be important for client satisfaction and repeat business.
  • Apparel businesses: Given your work in apparel and product distribution, we wanted to connect about local delivery support for recurring or batch shipments.
  • Electronics or parts suppliers: Given your work in electronics or parts distribution, reliable local delivery can help keep business customers supplied without unnecessary delays.
