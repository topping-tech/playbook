Executive Summary

From courier company to logistics growth architecture

In 2026, Topping Courier focuses on the local GTA market, but not with the traditional model of chasing many small clients. The core strategy is to find order-generation centers: companies that create a continuous delivery flow from one point to multiple destinations.

Core Logic

If Topping attracts importers, distributors, and distribution hubs instead of chasing scattered clients, each contract can generate multiple routes, multiple destinations, and recurring revenue.

"Don't chase the customer; find the center that generates customers and orders."

12-Month Roadmap

One-year roadmap

Q1

Build the foundation

Stabilize CRM, define Center Score, train negotiators, build a list of 300 centers, and reach 10 active clients.

Q2

Prove sales execution

Increase meeting volume, close recurring contracts, focus on medical/food/wholesale, and reach 20 to 25 active clients.

Q3

Increase route density

Build route density across GTA zones, reduce operating costs, produce customer case studies, and reach 35 active clients.

Q4

Prepare for scale

Reach 50 active clients, build a management dashboard, standardize the playbook, and prepare year-two expansion.

Risk Analysis

Risks and controls

Liquidity risk

Control: focus on prepaid/weekly billing contracts, reduce fixed costs, and scale driver capacity in stages.

Slow sales risk

Control: enforce Center Score, daily negotiator reporting, and remove weak leads before team time is consumed.

Operational risk

Control: start with limited zones, increase density, and deploy drivers based on real demand.

Market trust risk

Control: deliver professional proposals, case studies, clear communication guarantees, and transparent reporting.

Conclusion

Year-one objective: prove the model, not inflate the story

In year one, Topping should not try to capture every market. The realistic target is to build a repeatable sales and operations system, win 50 active B2B clients, create stable cash flow, and be ready to scale in year two.

Center → Node → Density → Recurring Delivery → Cash Flow → Scale
