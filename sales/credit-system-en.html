Topping Courier Monthly Credit & Bonus System - Complete Guide

Credit Calculation Algorithm - How It Adds Up
Monthly credit = (number of active customers x quantity weight) + (contract value x value weight)
Both dimensions are counted together - not only volume and not only customer count

Lead Acceptance Conditions

✅ Required conditions

1
Company name, contact number, and lead source - all three must be recorded in CRM
2
Clear source: Instagram · Facebook · Google · SEO · LinkedIn · Direct
3
Must be a B2B customer - business account, not personal
4
Must have a real courier/delivery need - not just curiosity

❌ Lead is rejected if

✕
Phone number is fake or invalid
✕
Customer was already registered in CRM by someone else (First Touch rule)
✕
Outside assigned geography nodes (Arshia = Node 1-2, Shayan = 3-5)
✕
High-risk industry without Founder approval (Refrigerated · Pallet-based · Emergency)
Payment Timeline - After Invoice
StageEventPaymentExplanation
Stage 1 Lead recorded in CRM — No payment at this stage
Stage 2 Meeting / contract signed — No invoice yet, still pending
Stage 3 First invoice paid Lead bonus is activated Collected pre-tax revenue ← calculation basis
Stage 4 2+ completed orders Sales/Closer commission Active Qualified Customer is confirmed
Stage 5 90 days active with no dispute +1 Retention bonus credit Added to that month’s credits
Golden Rule:
No collected money → no commission · No CRM proof → no commission
No margin → no commission · No qualified customer activity → no credit
Clawback: if the customer cancels within the first 30-90 days or does not pay the invoice, bonuses and commissions can be withheld or deducted. For large customers, Founder may approve phased payouts.
