Executive Summary

We are not building a sales team;
we are building a growth engine

Many courier companies leave growth to fragmented sales efforts. Topping Courier does not follow that path. We are building an engineered system where every lead, every call, every meeting, and every contract is captured, analyzed, and optimized within a measurable cycle.

The CEO leads business intelligence and data analytics: reading the market, identifying and scoring qualified leads, and handing them to the negotiation team. Sales then converts these opportunities into signed contracts and recurring revenue. CRM serves as the organizational memory of this process.

The end result is not just a single number; it is a repeatable architecture to reach 50 active B2B customers, maintain healthy cash flow, sustain stable revenue, and build geographic density that reduces per-package delivery cost over time.

"Growth is not accidental; growth is designed."

Hamid Barzin — CEO, Topping Courier Inc.
