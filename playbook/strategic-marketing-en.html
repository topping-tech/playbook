🔄 Outreach Flow — Roadmap 1 (SMB)
1
🔍 Find 70 companies from Hunter / LinkedIn

Each week, identify 70 companies from the current neighborhood + target industry. Data: name, website, LinkedIn, email, phone, decision-maker name.

2
📊 Analyze and score (0 to 10)

Based on: recurring potential, B2B fit, vehicle fit, risk level, batching potential, and service expectation — only scores of 8+ enter primary outreach.

3
🚚 Check current courier partnership

Do they already use a courier? Who are they working with? Find this from their website, LinkedIn posts, and Google reviews.

4
🔎 Review the company’s LinkedIn posts

What do they post? What challenges do they show? Use that insight to create targeted comments.

5
💬 Post a targeted LinkedIn comment

Write comments that show research, understanding, and value — not direct promotion.

✅ Replied to comment — same day
  • 📩 LinkedIn direct message the same day
  • 📧 Send a personalized email
  • 🗺️ Direct them to Google Business Profile for trust-building
  • 📅 Set a meeting (phone/address — not email)
❌ No reply — Follow-up
  • 💬 Second comment after 3 days
  • 📧 Send email 2 more days later
  • 📩 LinkedIn Direct Message
  • 📘 Facebook Message
  • ✈️ Telegram Message
  • 💬 WhatsApp Message
  • 🗂️ If still no response → archive for marketer phone call

🟣 Roadmap 2 — Large Brands (Enterprise)
Enterprise · Large Business
📌 Same 10 neighborhoods and same industries, but larger brands. Each week, identify 2 large brands through social media or Hunter. Key difference: instead of only contacting the company, connect directly with all decision-making members.
👥 Decision-makers to identify
  • Owner / CEO / Founder / President
  • Operations Manager / Director
  • Logistics Manager / Coordinator
  • Procurement Manager
  • Warehouse / Production Manager
  • Office Manager
  • Sales Manager / Customer Service Manager
🔍 Tools to find decision makers
  • LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Company + Role filter)
  • Hunter.io (corporate email)
  • Google: "Operations Manager [Company Name]"
  • LinkedIn Company Page → People
  • Instagram / Facebook Business Page
  • Website → About Us / Team

🔄 Outreach Flow — Roadmap 2 (Enterprise)

1
🏢 Run Roadmap 1 once at company level

Company LinkedIn comment, company email, and company Facebook page — one full pass.

2
👥 Identify all decision makers

Full list: name, title, personal LinkedIn, personal email, and phone (if possible).

3
📩 Personal LinkedIn outreach — first

Message: "Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] is active in [industry] — are you currently working with a courier company for deliveries?"

4
📧 Personal email — second

If there is no reply, send an email with the same angle. Focus on Topping advantages: dedicated team, higher speed, every package matters.

5
📘 Personal Facebook / ✈️ Telegram / 💬 WhatsApp — third

If there is still no reply, use their personal social channels.

6
💬 Comment on personal LinkedIn posts

Leave smart comments on their posts to get noticed, then send a direct message.

🎯 Roadmap 2 first message (example):
"Hi [Name], I saw that [Company] is active in [industry] in [neighborhood]. Are you currently using a courier for your product deliveries? Topping Courier works exclusively in B2B and delivers each package faster than competitors. Can we schedule a 15-minute call?"
✅ Replied
  • 📩 LinkedIn + 📧 follow-up email
  • 🗺️ Google Business for trust-building
  • 📅 Meeting setup (phone/address)
  • 📋 Prepare a company briefing document
❌ No response (no decision maker)
  • 🔄 Move to the next person in the DM list
  • 📘 Personal Facebook outreach
  • ✈️ Personal Telegram outreach
  • 💬 Personal WhatsApp outreach
  • 🗂️ Archive → marketer phone call
⚠️ Key Roadmap 2 difference: Company-level contact happens once. But each decision maker is approached individually — each follows a full outreach path.
🗂️ Tracking and CRM System
Without accurate tracking, 25+ parallel companies will become unmanageable. Track these fields for every lead.
📝 Required fields per lead
  • Company name + website
  • Neighborhood (GTA Zone)
  • Industry / phase
  • Decision maker name + title
  • Company LinkedIn + personal LinkedIn
  • Email (company + personal)
  • Phone number
  • Score 0 to 10
  • Current courier status
  • Last contact date
  • Channel used
  • Next step + date
📊 Pipeline stages (Status)
  • 🆕 New — recently added
  • 🔍 Researched — analyzed
  • 💬 Commented — comment posted
  • 📩 Messaged — message sent
  • 📧 Emailed — email sent
  • 🔄 Multi-channel — multiple channels active
  • 💬 Responded — replied
  • 📅 Meeting Set — meeting scheduled
  • ✅ Won — collaboration started
  • 🗂️ Archived — archived / marketer
🛠️ Recommended tools: Notion (free, simple), HubSpot Free, or Google Sheets with proper filters. Most important: update daily.
✍️ Message Structure (Template Framework)
💬 LinkedIn comment (sample structure)

✅ "[A point about the post] — in [industry], delivery challenges are usually [relevant challenge]. Interesting that [value point or smart question]."

❌ No direct promotion in comments

📩 First LinkedIn DM (sample)

"Hi [Name], I saw your comment — are you currently using a courier for product deliveries? Topping Courier is B2B-focused and much faster. Do you have 15 minutes?"

📧 First email (structure)
  • Subject: [Company Name] + Dedicated Courier Service
  • Short intro + what I saw about your business
  • One question: are you currently using a courier?
  • Topping key advantage: dedicated team, every package matters
  • CTA: 15-minute conversation
📋 Pre-meeting prep
  • Company briefing document
  • Review their Google reviews
  • Defined key questions
  • Initial proposal ready
  • Topping Google Business Profile ready
🔑 Topping core message across all channels: "Topping Courier has a dedicated B2B team — every package matters to us, and we deliver much faster than competitors."
❌ Never say: emergency courier, rush delivery, refrigerated, pallet, freight, heavy industrial
